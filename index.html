About Me

Howdy! I'm Dwarkanath Prabhu. I am a self-taught software developer. I am based out of Mumbai, India. I was born here and lived the first 22 years of my life here before moving to Delhi for work. Later I lived in College Station, TX for about 6 years where I completed my masters degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISEN) at Texas A&M University and later worked as a software engineer there till Jan 2022 when I moved back to Mumbai. My previous experience includes 2.5 years at a consulting firm and 1.5 years at 2 different startups. Prior to that I completed my undergraduate course in Mechanical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay.


I enjoy watching soccer, specifically Arsenal Football Club which I have been a fan of for 20 years now. In my first semester at Texas A&M, I worked as a soccer official for the Intramural Leagues. I also enjoy stand-up comedy – watching and at times, performing. Check out my comedy pages. On weekends, I like visiting parks. The cover photo above is from the trail at Bee Creek Park in College Station.


While studying at Texas A&M, I helped establish the Texas A&M Secular Student Alliance in 2017. It is the only atheist organization on campus.


Check out my blog and my social media profiles to know more about me.
